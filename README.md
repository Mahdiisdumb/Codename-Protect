# Codename-Protect

**Codename-Protect** is a survival horror game where your mission is simple but terrifying: **protect five kids and yourself from monsters**, using only your **flashlight**.  

The tension ramps up as monsters swarm, and it’s your skill and strategy that determine who survives the night.

Live demo: https://codename-protect.vercel.app/

## Gameplay

- **Objective:** Keep yourself and 5 kids alive from monster attacks  
- **Weapon:** A flashlight — aim carefully to scare or kill monsters  
- **Mechanics:**  
  - Monsters spawn randomly  
  - You must manage positioning and focus on protecting the kids  
  - Nighttime setting increases suspense  
- **Win Condition:** Survive 8 minutes
